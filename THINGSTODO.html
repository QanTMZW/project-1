What is Akihabara

Akihabara is a buzzing shopping hub famed for its electronics retailers, ranging from tiny stalls to vast department stores like Yodobashi Multimedia Akiba.

Map of Akihabara

Info

Akihabara is the central hub for hobby enthusiasts of all kinds. The many department electronic retailers also carry a huge variety of anime merchandise inside their many stores. All the promotional Anime material lands here in Akihabara first after conventions. There are many arcades hidden in every corner of this great city, with many machines worth your time and money for entertainment.
