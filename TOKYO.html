What is Tokyo

Tokyo is the world's largest metropolis, it is known for top-rated restaurants, the Shibuya Crossing, Imperial Palace, cherry blossoms, markets, and more. The shopping scene includes the Harajuku and Akihabara districts. Tokyo is also famous for its vending machines, cat cafes, museums, and sacred sites.

What can you do in TOKYO?

Out in Odaiba is the life-sized real-to-scale 1/1 Unicorn Gundam in front of Diver City Mall. Typically in Unicorn mode, the statue will transform from Unicorn mode to a more recognizable Gundam form, its Destroy Mode. During evenings, you can catch projection screening shows projected onto the mall, with all special effects as if the Gundam is coming to life!

Over in Asakusa, the famous Sensoji Temple is where the word sacred is most defined here. The place dates back some 1,300 years in Japan's history and it is famous for its entrance gate named Kaminarimon which stands out for its abnormally large red lantern. After the lantern, there are lines of small food shops, various of which that sell gluttinous rice cakes and seaweed snacks.
